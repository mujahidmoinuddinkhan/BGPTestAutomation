User Story 3 (EPIC) - Form Submission

Test Scenarios:

Verify that after filling all mandatory fields in all form sections, clicking "Review" takes the applicant to a read-only summary page.
Verify that missing mandatory inputs trigger validation errors and redirect to the section with missing details.
Verify that the read-only summary page contains all previously filled details.
Verify that checking the "Consent and Acknowledgement" checkbox allows submission and displays a success message.
Verify that the success message includes an Application Ref ID and the receiving Agency is 'Enterprise Singapore.'
Verify that upon submission, the application is shown under the 'Processing' tab on the 'My Grants' dashboard.
