User Story 1 - Eligibility Section

Test Scenarios:

Verify that all five eligibility questions are displayed on the Eligibility page.
Verify that each question can be answered with "Yes" or "No" using radio buttons.
Verify that answering "No" to any question displays a warning message.
Verify that clicking the link in the warning message launches the FAQ page.
Verify that clicking "Save" saves the applicant's inputs, and refreshing the page reloads the saved values.
