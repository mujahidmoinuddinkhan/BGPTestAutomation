User Story 2 - Contact Details Section

Test Scenarios:

Verify that the Contact Details page contains inputs for the Main Contact Person and Letter of Offeree.
Verify that Mailing Address input auto-populates "Blk/Hse No." and "Street details" from an external API.
Verify that the "Same as registered address in Company Profile" checkbox auto-populates Mailing Address.
Verify that the "Same as main contact person" option populates Letter of Offeree details.
Verify that clicking "Save" saves the applicant's inputs and reloading the page reloads the saved values.
